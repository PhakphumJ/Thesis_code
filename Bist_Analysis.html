Thesis_Business_Analysis

Explanation

In this document/code, I will examine key dimensions of business () firms. The main goal is to see whether there are differences in key dimensions across different regions. The main characteristics that I will examine are:

  1. Firm size (Number of workers)
  1. 2555: b05 + b10
  2. 2565: B07 + B14
  1. Foreign Investment
  1. 2555: a09
  2. 2565: A11
  1. Sales:
  1. 2555: d01 or d03 (hotels and guesthouses)
  2. 2565: D01 or D03 (hotels and guesthouses)
  1. Fixed Assets:
  1. 2555: f06
  2. 2565: E08
  1. R&D Cost:
  1. 2555: c09
  2. 2565: C09
  1. Number of Firms:
  1. 2555: sum of weights
  2. 2565: sum of weights
  1. Estimated TFP (estimated using sales, wage bill, and fixed assets).
  2. Sales per worker. I donâ€™t use value added because it is tricky for service sectors.
  1. 2555: (d01 or d03) / Number of workers
  2. 2565: (D01 or D03) / Number of workers
  1. K/L
  1. 2555: f06 / number of workers
  2. 2565: E08 / number of workers

Before analysing, I will trim the data by removing firms with sales lower than 6,000, fixed assets lower than 10,000, firms with zero workers.

I also remove firms with top and bottom 1% of sales per worker or top and bottom 1% of K/L.

Moreover, we remove obseravations detected to be duplicates based on seven variables: (i) years in operation, (ii) total employment, (iii) wage compensation, (iv) raw materials, (v) initial raw material stocks, (vi) initial finished product stocks, and (vii) initial fixed assets
