Thesis_INDUS_Analysis

Explanation

In this document/code, I will examine key dimensions of manafacturing firms. The main goal is to see whether there are differences in key dimensions across different regions. The main characteristics that I will examine are:

  1. Firm size (Number of employees)
  1. 2540: (A26 + A31)
  2. 2550: (A24 + A28)
  3. 2560: (B04 + B08)
  1. Foreign Investment
  1. 2540: A05
  2. 2550: A09
  3. 2560: A09
  1. Exporting
  1. 2540: A11
  2. 2550: A15
  3. 2560: A12
  1. Sales
  1. 2540: F01
  2. 2550: G01
  3. 2560: D01
  1. Profit before tax (Total income - Cost of sales - Administrative expenses - Costs of raw materials, production, goods, work done by others - wages bill)
  1. 2540: F09 - E01 - E02 - C01 - C02 - C03 - C04 - B04 - B08 - B09 - B10 - B11
  2. 2550: G01 + G13 - E01 - E02 - C01 - C02 - C03 - C04 - B05 - B10
  3. 2560: D01 + D13 - C21 - C41 - C06 - B19 - B24
  1. Total Fixed Assets
  1. 2540: H07
  2. 2550: I08
  3. 2560:
  1. Problems of operation
  1. 2560: J02, J03, J04, J05, J06, J07, J08, J09, J10, J11, J12, J13, J14, J15, J16, J17 –> Group into:
  1. Low Demand or Marketing Problems: J02, J10, J11
  2. Labor Shortages: J05
  3. Lack of capital: J09
  4. Technology Problem: J06, J07
  1. Needs from the government
  1. 2560: J19, J20, J21, J22, J23, J24, J25, J26, J27, J28, J29, J30, J31, J32, J33, J34 –> Group into:
  1. Skilled_Labor_support: J19
  2. Productivity_support: J20, J32
  3. Capital_Support: J21, J22, J23, J32
  4. Output_Market_Support: J25, J30
  1. R&D Costs
  1. 2540: No info
  2. 2550: E28
  3. 2560: C32
  1. Number of firms
  1. 2540: ??
  2. 2550: sum of weights
  3. 2560: sum of weights
  1. Estimated TFP (estimated using sales, wage bill, and fixed assets).
  2. Value-added/Employee: Value added is calculated as sales - cost of raw materials + change in inventory of raw materials.
  1. 2550: (G01 + (D05 - D01) - C01)/(A24 + A28)
  2. 2560: (D01 + (C12 – C07) – C01)/(B04 + B08)
  1. K/L
  1. 2550: I08/(A24 + A28)
  2. 2560: E16/(B04 + B08)
